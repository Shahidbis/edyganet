  <firstparagraph><![CDATA[McLEAN, VA - Gannett Co., Inc. (NYSE: GCI) will provide a live webcast of a question-and-answer session with Gannett CFO Victoria Harker at the Noble Financial Capital Markets’ Eleventh Annual Equity Conference at 1:30 p.m. (ET) on Tuesday, January 20.

To listen to the live webcast, go to www.gannett.com. On www.gannett.com, a link to the webcast will be under "Company News." The webcast also can be accessed through the Noble Financial web sites, www.noblefcm.com or www.nobleresearch.com/D2/2015.htm. Please allow at least 10 minutes prior to the start of the presentation to access the links before the presentations begin. To access the webcast, a Microsoft SilverLight viewer (a free download from the presentation link) is needed. The webcast and presentation also will be archived on www.gannett.com for 90 days following the event.

<b>About Gannett</b>
Gannett Co., Inc. (NYSE: GCI) is an international media and marketing solutions company that informs and engages more than 110 million people every month through its powerful network of broadcast, digital, mobile and publishing properties. Our portfolio of trusted brands offers marketers unmatched local-to-national reach and customizable, innovative marketing solutions across any platform. Gannett is committed to connecting people – and the companies who want to reach them – with their interests and communities. For more information, visit www.gannett.com.]]></firstparagraph>
